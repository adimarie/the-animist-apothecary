The Concept

A living map of where the work is

This is a living calendar and migratory route — showing where Adi is, when, and why. Not just a schedule, but a map of sacred intention: for planning gatherings, finding places to speak and teach, connecting with lands that are calling, hosting salons, gathering around firesides.

A place to bridge it all together — the solo healing work and the collective ceremonial work, the personal transformation and the planetary tending. Because they are not separate. They never were.

Earth Medicine

Eco-Immersions

Journeying not for ourselves, but to connect with the spirits of the living waters and land — attending to the interdimensional aspects of our many shared worlds. These are ceremonial pilgrimages to places of ecological significance, held in service to the land itself.

Gathering

Community Firesides

In-person gatherings around the fire — story, song, reflection, and the ancient art of being together in circle. Firesides are open community events held seasonally in Topanga Canyon and at various locations along the migratory route. No experience necessary; only presence.
