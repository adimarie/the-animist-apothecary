Collective Healing

Ceremonial & Ritual Groups

Because the work of tending the life web is communal.

The Offering

The power of collective intention

There are things that can only happen in community. The individual work is essential — it is the root. But when people come together in ceremony with genuine intention, something amplifies. The field between people becomes a medicine in itself.

Ceremonial & Ritual Groups take several forms: themed trajectory programs (4—12 sessions) where a consistent group moves through a shared arc of transformation; seasonal ceremonies aligned to the turning points of the year; and ceremonial training intensives for those developing their own facilitation practice.

When a group of people gathers in genuine ceremony — with their whole selves, their real questions, their actual grief and longing — something happens that can't be manufactured or explained. It is one of the most powerful medicines I know. — Adi Marie

Types of Group Offerings

Themed Group Trajectories

4—12 session programs for consistent groups. Current themes: The Underworld & Return, Ancestral Healing for Modern People, Embodied Sovereignty, The Mythic Body. Groups meet every 2—4 weeks.

Seasonal Ceremonies

Ceremonies aligned to solstices, equinoxes, and significant turning points of the seasonal wheel. Single-session, open enrollment, designed to mark the threshold moments of the year.

Eco-Immersion Groups

Ceremonial gatherings in nature — on the land, at the water, in the hills of Topanga or at sacred sites further afield. Oriented toward service to the living world.

Private Group Engagements

Custom ceremonial programs for organizations, communities of practice, therapy collectives, and medicine communities seeking ceremonial culture as part of their life.
