Sacred Reciprocity

A sliding scale rooted in mutual care

The sliding scale at The Animist Apothecary is not charity. It is not a discount. It is a practice of sacred reciprocity — an acknowledgment that healing work exists within an ecosystem of relationship, and that the exchange between practitioner and participant must honor both.

When you choose your tier, you are not simply selecting a price. You are making a statement about your relationship to this work, your current means, and your willingness to participate in a system where those with more help sustain access for those with less.

The Four Tiers

Patron

You are choosing to invest at a level that directly sustains this practice and makes lower-tier sessions possible for others.

Abundance

This rate reflects a comfortable investment in your healing work, honoring both your means and the practitioner's offering.

Community

A supported rate for those navigating financial transitions while still prioritizing their ceremonial and healing work.

Accessibility

Reserved for those experiencing genuine financial hardship. Limited slots available each season.

If none of these tiers are accessible to you right now, please reach out. I would rather find a path forward together than have finances be the reason you don't receive the care you need.
