Personal Immersions

Extended containers for those ready to go all the way in.

The Offering

Radical presence. Spiritual reclamation.

A Personal Immersion is exactly what it sounds like: a full, complete submersion in the work. One to five days of continuous ceremonial space — held with extraordinary care, calibrated entirely to your threshold, and oriented toward the kind of transformation that you can feel in your bones for the rest of your life.

These are bespoke experiences. No two are the same. They are designed in conversation with you before they begin — drawing from your specific situation, your edges, your longings, and your resistances — and then held with total devotion as they unfold.

Immersions typically include a combination of Shamanic Bodywork, Sacred Breath sessions, Ancestral Divinations, ceremonial work, time in the land, and integration sessions. Held in Topanga Canyon or at a location of significance to your journey.

Sometimes the most loving thing you can give yourself is an uninterrupted season of going in. Not checking the phone, not managing the day — just going in, all the way, with someone skilled enough to hold the edges. That is what an Immersion offers.

Immersions are for those at genuine thresholds — standing before a major life change, in the aftermath of significant loss, or simply ready for the kind of deep reset that a week of vacation cannot provide.

What a Personal Immersion May Include

Sacred Breath Sessions

Deep ceremonial openings for expanded states.

Shamanic Bodywork

Hands-on somatic and energetic healing.

Ancestral Divination

Readings and transmissions for clarity.

Land Connection

Time in Topanga Canyon's sacred landscape.

Ritual & Ceremony

Custom ceremonial containers throughout.

Integration Sessions

Guidance to weave insights into life.

Duration: 1 to 5 days. All immersions are designed through a detailed pre-immersion consultation. Post-immersion follow-up included.
