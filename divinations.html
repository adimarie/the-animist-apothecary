The Unseen Worlds

Ancestral & Elemental Divinations

A mirror held by those who walked before you.

The Offering

Clarity from the other side of the veil

The ancestral and elemental realms are not distant or mythological. They are alive — present in the patterns of our lives, in the recurring themes, the unexplained pulls, the inexplicable resistances. A divination is a technology for listening to what those realms are saying.

Adi Marie is an initiated diviner in the Dagara tradition of West Africa — a lineage that carries ancient, tested technologies for ancestral contact, elemental medicine, and the reading of the forces that shape a life. She is also a naturally gifted medium and oracle, with an innate capacity to receive and transmit communication from the unseen worlds.

Her readings are specific. They speak to the actual patterns and forces at work in your life — naming what is there, offering perspective from the ancestral and elemental viewpoints, and pointing toward the kinds of tending and action that will bring greater alignment and clarity.

The ancestors are not silent. The elements are not indifferent. They have been trying to reach you. A divination creates the conditions for that contact — and for receiving what they've been trying to say. — Adi Marie

Types of Readings

Ancestral Divination

Drawing from the Dagara lineage, these readings open a channel to the ancestral field — addressing what your lineage is asking, what is trying to heal, and what gifts and burdens have been transmitted through the blood.

Elemental Divination

A reading of the elemental forces at work in your life — Water, Fire, Earth, Mineral, and Nature — and how their balance or imbalance is expressing in your current situation.

Oracle & Mediumship

Adi's natural mediumistic gifts allow for direct communication with those who have passed, spirit guides, and entities in the unseen world. Responsive to what arises; directed by who shows up to speak.

All sessions include time for questions and integration conversation. Recordings available. Sessions are typically 90 minutes to 2 hours. Available virtually worldwide and in person in Topanga Canyon.
