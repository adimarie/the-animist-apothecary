A Metamorphosis Has Been Underway

From bodywork to
deep soul tending

After 16 years of service as a Somatic Touch Therapist and Hands-on Spiritual Practitioner — my work through Bodywork & Botanicals has come into a new evolution of specialized offerings and curation.

The many people who came seeking respite on the massage table almost always had deeper wounds that needed attending to — expressing as physical symptoms of disharmony within their system as pain.

After over a decade of devoted study, initiation, and maturation as an answer to the deeper calling of my gifts in service to the world, I now have a richer set of specialized skills to provide the much needed deep work to bring about life changing contact with the inner domains of Spirit and Self.

What has always been here remains: deep somatic tending, sacred space, relational care, and the devoted art of helping people return to themselves. And something new is arriving, too.
Bodywork & Botanicals has become the next evolution of shamanic care: The Animist Apothecary
The Greater Calling

Healing the earth grid.
One ceremony at a time.

The great work of our time is not only personal — it is planetary. Adi Marie is cultivating communities of ceremonialists, building collective healing containers, and weaving systems of sacred resourcing in service to this precious planet and all sacred kin.

How to Begin

Your path forward,
step by step

The work begins with a simple, honest inquiry. From there, a path reveals itself.

Awakening or Healing? · Solo or Groups? · Once or Ongoing?
01

Intake & Inquiry

A paid Intake & Inquiry session (Pláticas rate) to feel the resonance, share your needs, assess readiness, and outline a path forward together. This is a substantive consultation — not a brief intro call.

02

Choose Your Path

Together we identify whether a Solo Acolyte intensive, group trajectory, or individual session best meets your readiness.

03

Commit & Begin

A deposit opens the container and signals your readiness. Dates are set. The work is engaged with full intention.

04

Aftercare & Integration

The work continues in the weaving. Integration practices and follow-up support are woven throughout every offering.
