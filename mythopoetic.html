Mythopoetic Being

The stories we carry become the lives we live

Each of us carries an intimate, and completely unique, interpretive map of the world and our place within it. This is an intricately personal weave, composed of the stories we have been given — through experience and inheritance.

The waking dream we live within is held together with the mortar of the stories we echo within ourselves, and thus, consequentially, they become the living stories we believe define our lives.

Together, through deep presencing, we examine the myths and the folklore that have been the bones your dissatisfied life has been built upon. We respectfully, and compassionately give them the space for expression, catharsis, meaning, context and resolution.

We transmute the texts, and explore the rewrites that bring you into the life you want to be living. More importantly, my desire is to imbue you with the skills and confidence to navigate this terrain and embrace your life fully — with the power and agency of embodying the temporal Narrative 'I' from a place of gentle equanimity. We do this by connecting with primordial and primal powers for medicine, healing, grace, and guidance — through remembering and recontextualizing the scope of our individuated identities within the epic, infinite and eternal tapestry of life itself.

The Developmental Arc

A 12-session ceremonial progression

This Arc is a 12-session progression, typically held within a single season — though it can be expanded into 6, 9, 12, 18, or 24 month commitments.

It is built as a continuity container — meaning we don't do the deep ceremonial opening without the steady thread of integration running alongside it. Because once this work begins, it illuminates inner shadows. Not to punish, but to be loved, metabolized, and repatterned.

This is intensive, layered work. It is for those deeply committed to self-actualization, spiritual sovereignty, and the kind of healing that changes the shape of a life.

6 Sacred Breath Sessions

3 hours each — deep inner work and ceremonial opening. Full ceremonial containers for expanded states, ancestral contact, and deep somatic release.

6 Guidance & Counsel Sessions

2 hours each — integration, nervous system tending, life-application, and repatterning. Weaves ceremonial insights into daily life.

Recommended pacing: every other week — enough space to integrate, close enough the thread stays warm.

Available as Solo Acolyte Intensives (3, 6, 9, 12, 18, or 24 month commitments) and themed Group Trajectories.

The sacred tools of your becoming

Methods in Practice

The Ouroboros Cycle

The living spiral of transformation. Each phase feeds the next — the serpent swallows its own tail and is reborn. This is how change that lasts is made.

I

Preparation & Priming

Connect with and cultivate intentions through lessons, assignments, and personal sessions. Prime the psyche — addressing inherited blueprints, identifying limiting beliefs and emotional wounds. Build a foundation of somatic and embodied practices to support peak emotional states.

II

Transformation & Alchemy

The peak: ritual and medicine ceremonies. Access extraordinary states of consciousness, bring all modalities together. Turn to the realms of Spirit through Earth-based modalities — divinations, channeling, ritual, offerings, prayer, and the cultivation of spiritual fortitude.

III

Grounding & Integration

The recombobulation phase. Proper aftercare for lasting positive impact. Tend to what's been revealed. Intensively integrate new awarenesses and ground the changes from your intentions into a life you recognise as your own.

∞

The cycle returns — each revolution deeper, each season more alive.
